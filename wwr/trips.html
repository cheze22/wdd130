Featured Rafting Trips

Family Float Adventure

Difficulty: Easy

Duration: 3 hours | Distance: 8 miles

Perfect for families and first-time rafters! This gentle float takes you through scenic canyon views with Class I-II rapids. Enjoy wildlife sightings, swimming opportunities, and a relaxing journey down the river. Our guides provide interesting geological and historical information along the way. Suitable for ages 5 and up. Includes all safety equipment, professional guide, and riverside snacks.

Price: $85 per person

Thunder Canyon Express

Difficulty: Moderate

Duration: 5 hours | Distance: 12 miles

Our most popular adventure! Experience the perfect blend of exciting Class III rapids and calmer stretches for catching your breath. Navigate through Thunder Canyon's dramatic rock formations and enjoy thrilling drops and waves. This trip includes challenging rapids like "The Washing Machine" and "Devil's Staircase." Perfect for adventurers looking for excitement without extreme difficulty. Ages 12 and up. Includes lunch, all equipment, and experienced guides.

Price: $145 per person

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Difficulty: Extreme

Duration: Full Day (8 hours) | Distance: 18 miles

For experienced rafters seeking the ultimate adrenaline rush! Tackle Class IV-V rapids including the legendary "Los Dientes del Diablo" (Devil's Teeth) and "Big Mallard" drops. This full-day expedition tests your skills with continuous challenging rapids, steep drops, and technical maneuvers. Requires previous rafting experience and excellent physical condition. Ages 16 and up with parental consent (18+ recommended). Includes gourmet riverside lunch, premium equipment, and our most experienced guides.

Price: $225 per person
