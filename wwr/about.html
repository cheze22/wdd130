Wild Rapids Rafting

At Wild Rapids Rafting, our mission is to provide unforgettable white water adventures while maintaining the highest safety standards. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the thrill of conquering nature's most exciting rapids in a safe and supportive environment. Our experienced guides are passionate about sharing their love for the river and creating memories that will last a lifetime.

History

Founded in 1995 by river enthusiasts Jake Morrison and Sarah Chen, Wild Rapids Rafting began as a small family operation with just two rafts and a dream to share their passion for white water adventures. Over nearly three decades, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted rafting companies, having safely guided over 50,000 adventurers down some of the most spectacular rivers in the area. Our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and creating exceptional experiences has earned us numerous awards and the loyalty of countless returning customers.
