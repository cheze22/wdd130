History

Founded in 1995 by river enthusiasts Jake Morrison and Sarah Chen, Wild Rapids Rafting began as a small family operation with just two rafts and a dream to share their passion for white water adventures. Over nearly three decades, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted rafting companies, having safely guided over 50,000 adventurers down some of the most spectacular rivers in the area.

Our Mission

At Wild Rapids Rafting, our mission is to provide unforgettable white water adventures while maintaining the highest safety standards. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the thrill of conquering nature's most exciting rapids in a safe and supportive environment.

Meet Our Team

Our team of experienced guides and staff are passionate about sharing their love for the river. With decades of combined experience, they ensure every trip is safe, fun, and memorable for all our adventurers.
